Biryani

Biryani is a popular South Asian mixed rice dish made with aromatic spices, basmati rice, and marinated meat or vegetables.

      Types of Biryani:

    • Hyderabadi Biryani
    • Lucknowi Biryani
    • Sindhi Biryani
    • Chicken Biryani
    • Veg Biryani

      • Preparation:

      • Cook basmati rice separately.
      • Marinate meat or vegetables in spices and yogurt.
      • Cook the marinated mixture in a pot.
      • Layer the cooked rice and mixture in a pot.
      • Steam the biryani until the flavors meld together.

Parotta

Parotta originated in the southern Indian state of Tamil Nadu, particularly in the city of Madurai.


Preparation

    • Mix wheat flour, water, and salt to form a dough.
    • Knead the dough for several minutes.
    • Rest the dough for a few hours.
    • Divide the dough into small balls
    • Roll out each ball into a thin circle.
    • Apply oil and fold the dough to create layers
    • Cook the parotta on a hot griddle or tava.

Fish Fry

Fish fry is a popular dish in many coastal cuisines, especially in India, Southeast Asia, and the Mediterranean. It's a simple yet flavorful preparation of fish coated with spices and fried until crispy.

Types of Fish Fry

    • South Indian-style fish fry (with spices and chilies)
    • Goan-style fish fry (with coconut and tamarind)
    • Bengali-style fish fry (with mustard oil and spices)
    • Mediterranean-style fish fry (with herbs and lemon)
